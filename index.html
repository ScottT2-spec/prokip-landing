The Real Problem

Many African Business Owners Are Working Too Hard Inside Their Own Business

You open early. You close late. You check sales yourself. You monitor staff yourself. You answer every small question. You stay because you are not fully sure what will happen if you leave.

  • Staff say one thing. Stock records say another thing. Cash does not always match sales.
  • Customers owe money, but records are not clear. Expenses are increasing, but nobody can explain why.
  • Goods enter the store, but nobody can confidently say what is left.
  • Reports come late — or not at all. And when there is a problem, everybody has an explanation. But there is no clear record.
  • That is where many business owners lose money. Not because the business is bad — but because it depends too much on people and not enough on a system.

And even after all that stress, you may still be asking:

  • "Can I really trust what my staff are telling me?"
  • "Is my stock complete?"
  • "Are we making profit or just making sales?"
  • "Where is the money going?"

If this sounds familiar, you are not alone. Many businesses are growing, but the owner is still trapped in daily operations.

Prokip helps you change that.
